About Our Practice

At Cates Dental Services, we've been caring for families since 2005. Our modern facility offers state-of-the-art technology in a comfortable, welcoming environment.

We believe in preventive care and education as the keys to optimal dental health. We strive to provide dental health care rather than disease care, ensuring you maintain a healthy smile for life.

Our Dental Services

Preventive Care

Regular cleanings, exams, and x-rays to maintain your oral health and prevent problems before they start.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Teeth whitening, veneers, and bonding to give you the beautiful smile you've always wanted.

Restorative Services

Fillings, crowns, bridges, and implants to restore function and appearance to damaged teeth.

Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle, compassionate dental care for children to establish lifelong healthy habits.
